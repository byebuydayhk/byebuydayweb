ByeBuyDayHK Pledge
Objectives Over the series of protests that have started in June, the Hong Kong government has demonstrated a blatant disregard for the people’s will and a total lack of moral decency. Such as colluding with the police and triad groups, infringing Hong Kong citizen’s rights by silencing them against their will with violence and political prosecution. As a result, Hong Kong is now enshrouded by white terror. For the above reasons, we are launching this city wide economic non-cooperative movement, where we pledge to minimise our expenditures to strongly condemn and pressure the government into responding to the five demands. Pledger’s obligations • I pledge to be an ethical consumer who will limit my spending on necessities only and will not purchase any goods produced or sold by manufacturers or shops which have endorsed human rights abuse by the Hong Kong government.
